Surviving the odds of life is a technical book that gives the general public a picture of how an individual should package himself in the midst of the decay in the country. It also x-rays the rewards of hardworks and consistency in the pursuit of personal goals irrespective of one's background through the experiences of an orphan(Uzoma) who nevertheless survived all the odds. This book is a typical illustration of African nations. It highlights some abstract worms that can eat up the polity and politics of any nation and as well tips to imbibe for nation-building/development. If you crave for good politics, then this book might be for you!! This book is purely descriptive, mind-blower, thought-provoker and a dream-developer!
